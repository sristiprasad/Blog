# Series: Design Requirement Summary: Khel Tic Tac
<hr>
## How to use design memory to create theme wireframes

## Project Overview
A premium, culturally-rooted Tic Tac Toe mobile game featuring high-fidelity, intricate line-art iconography based on South Indian temple architecture and traditional motifs.

## Visual Identity & Palette
*   **Background:** Royal Parrot Green (a vibrant, deep emerald/forest hybrid, e.g., `#0B5D3B`).
*   **Primary Accent (Gold):** `#D4AF37` or `#C9A227` for main player coins and titles.
*   **Secondary Accent (Silver):** Bright Silver with an embossed effect for the second player coins.
*   **Grid Lines:** Muted Gold (`#C9A227` at ~40-50% opacity), thin stroke (~1-1.5px).

## Layout & Composition
*   **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 Vertical Mobile-First.
*   **Game Grid:** 
    *   3x3 square grid centered horizontally.
    *   Positioned slightly above the vertical midpoint.
    *   Reduced size for a focused, elegant feel.
*   **Title:** "KHEL TIC TAC"
    *   Positioned centrally below the grid.
    *   Typography: High-contrast Serif (e.g., Cinzel, Trajan, or Noto Serif).
    *   Styling: ALL CAPS, wide letter spacing (tracking), Semi-Bold weight.

## Iconography (Game Coins)

### Player 1: Gold Temple Head (Gopuram)
*   **Theme:** South Indian Tirupati Temple head.
*   **Form:** Intricate, designer stepped pyramid/Gopuram structure.
*   **Details:**
    *   Stacked tiers with varying widths/heights (non-regular).
    *   Vertical ribbing and decorative accents for an "embossed" look.
    *   Pronounced "Kalash" peak at the top.
    *   Stroke: Thin gold line-art (1.5-2px), slightly rounded joins.
    *   Symmetry: Perfect vertical symmetry.

### Player 2: Silver Windy Swirl
*   **Theme:** South Indian themed organic swirl/ocean wave.
*   **Form:** Abstract, free-flowing spiral motion.
*   **Details:**
    *   2-3 overlapping curved strokes with trailing arcs.
    *   Rhythmic, hand-drawn aesthetic with intentional slight asymmetry.
    *   Color: Bright Silver with an embossed visual effect.
    *   Stroke: Consistent 1.5-2px thickness with rounded caps for softness.

## Win State / Strike Line
*   **Color:** Bright Gold (`#F2C94C`) with a subtle glow effect.
*   **Thickness:** ~3-4px (clearly thicker than grid lines).
*   **Placement:** Straight line passing directly through the centers of the winning coins.
*   **Layering:** Coins sit visually on top of the strike line.

## UI Principles
*   **Minimalism:** No heavy headers, task bars, or cluttered UI elements.
*   **Texture:** Use of "embossed" line-art to create a sense of depth and physical premium quality.
*   **Cultural Roots:** Icons must feel "rooted" and strategic rather than generic.
